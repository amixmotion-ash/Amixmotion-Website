ABOUT

After 14 years as a creative in digital design, I discovered my
passion for visual storytelling while traveling through Asia. Now
based in London — and always happy to travel — I specialise in
videography and editing that blends design sensibility with cinematic
technique. From creative concepts and shot planning to dynamic
cinematography and polished edits, I craft engaging narratives that
bring brands, events, and ideas to life through compelling visual content.
